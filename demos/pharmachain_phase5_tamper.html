📚 Why Can't They Fix It?
A smart attacker might try to re-hash the tampered block and update its hash. But this still breaks the chain because:
1. The next block still stores the original hash as its prev_hash
2. Fixing block N forces them to fix block N+1 too, and so on
3. To fix the whole chain, they'd need all participants' private keys — impossible
✓ This is why blockchain is called immutable
